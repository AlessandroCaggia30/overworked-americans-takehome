- **Caballero, R. J. & Hammour, M. L. (1998).** *Jobless Growth: Appropriability, Factor Substitution, and Unemployment*. Gertler & Rogoff.
- **Boeri, T. & Garibaldi, P. (2007).** *Two Tier Reforms of Employment Protection: A Honeymoon Effect?* Economic Journal 117(521), F357–F385. https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1468-0297.2007.02060.x — *Used in: dual-labor-market reforms and insider/outsider welfare incidence.*
- **Card, D. (1992).** *Do Minimum Wages Reduce Employment? A Case Study of California, 1987–89*. Industrial and Labor Relations Review 46(1), 38–54. https://doi.org/10.2307/2524736 — *Used in: empirical anchor for wage-floor / markup effects on employment.* **Venue correction:** the prompt listed AER; the canonical Card 1992 paper is in ILRR (the AER companion is Card & Krueger 1994). The ILRR piece is the correct match for the "minimum wages and union markups" framing.
